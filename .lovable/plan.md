

## ARIA — Web3 Landing Page Hero Section

### What We're Building
A cinematic, full-screen hero section that immediately communicates "production-grade" and creates a WOW moment for hackathon judges. This is the front door to ARIA.

### Key Components

1. **Fullscreen Video Background** — Looping muted video with 50% black overlay for that premium dark aesthetic

2. **Navbar** — Clean horizontal layout with:
   - "LOGOIPSUM" wordmark (left)
   - 4 nav links with dropdown chevrons (hidden on mobile)
   - "Join Waitlist" pill button with glowing top-edge effect and layered border construction

3. **Hero Content (centered)** — 
   - "Early access" badge pill with glowing dot and date
   - Large gradient-text heading: "Web3 at the Speed of Experience" (white-to-transparent gradient)
   - Subtitle paragraph at 70% opacity
   - White CTA "Join Waitlist" button with glow effect

4. **Typography** — General Sans font from Fontshare loaded globally

5. **Responsive Design** — Nav links collapse below `md`, heading scales from 56px → 36px, padding adjusts for mobile

### Design Details
- Pure black (#000) background
- Layered button construction with 0.6px borders and top-edge glow streaks
- Text gradient using `background-clip: text` technique
- Smooth, premium feel throughout
